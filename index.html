This is My Review Site

This is a project created with the goal of practicing my skills with HTML, CSS and JAVASCRIPT, I'm learning how to use Parallax effects and decided to make a site where I will review movies, series and video games.

The reviews are based on my objective perspective, and many of them are also subjective. In either case, I'll mention it in the text. The works shown are only those I've seen, with a rating from 1 to 10.
